TRANSFORMATION METHODOLOGY

The NextGen Business Operations Platform

A structured, data-driven approach to Business Operations readiness, operating model design, transformation delivery, and steady-state operations management.

FOUR-PILLAR FRAMEWORK
Assess → Design → Transform → Operate

Every engagement follows the same rigorous framework — adapted to the domain, scale, and maturity of the organisation.

01
Assess
4–8 weeks
  • Business Operations Operational Maturity Assessment (20 questions per domain)
  • FTE mapping — retained vs outsourced by function
  • Cost baseline by function and process
  • Technology landscape inventory and integration readiness
  • Process volumetrics and KPI benchmarking
Business Operations Readiness Report with maturity score (0–80) and prioritised gap list
02
Design
8–16 weeks
  • Target Operating Model definition by function
  • Retained/outsourced FTE model by function and process tier
  • SLA and governance framework design
  • Business Operations provider selection support and scoring
  • Technology integration architecture
Target Operating Model document and Business Operations transition blueprint
03
Transform
6–18 months
  • Wave-based process transition by geography or business unit
  • Change management and communications programme
  • Process redesign and SOP documentation
  • Technology configuration and user acceptance testing
  • Parallel run and hypercare period
Live Business Operations operations with signed-off SLAs and transition completion report
04
Operate
Ongoing
  • Continuous KPI monitoring and monthly performance reporting
  • Root-cause analysis for exceptions and SLA breaches
  • Continuous improvement cycle with prioritised backlog
  • Contract performance management and governance
  • Annual maturity re-assessment against updated benchmarks
Steady-state operations dashboard and continuous improvement register
PLATFORM CAPABILITIES

What the Platform Provides

Six integrated capabilities spanning the full Business Operations transformation lifecycle — from first assessment to ongoing operations management.

Business Operations Maturity Assessment
60-question diagnostic across Procurement (Source-to-Pay), Finance (R2R, AP/AR), and HR (H2R, Payroll). Scored 0–80 per domain with an instant maturity band and structured gap analysis.
FTE & Cost Modelling
Map your retained and outsourced headcount by function. Benchmark against industry norms. Identify redeployment and cost reduction opportunities across the operating model.
Transformation Pipeline
Track active engagements by phase (Assess / Design / Transform / Operate) and domain. Monitor progress, risks, and milestones across the full transformation portfolio.
Business Operations Playbooks & Accelerators
Ready-to-deploy process frameworks, SOP templates, governance models, and transformation toolkits for HR, Finance, and Procurement engagements.
ROI & Value Tracking
Monitor FTE redeployment, cycle time reduction, cost savings, and compliance improvements. Build and update the business case continuously throughout the engagement lifecycle.
Benchmarking & Proof Points
Industry benchmarks and Client Zero performance data to anchor your business case and validate transformation targets against real-world Business Operations outcomes.
